Why Epsilon?

Every day, millions of Indians panic when symptoms appear — overwhelmed by unreliable search results, diagnostic confusion, and expensive consultations for simple conditions.

At the same time, rural users trust local remedies first. Many are more comfortable explaining symptoms in their native language. Younger generations are disconnected from Ayurvedic wisdom. And no existing tool bridges all of these gaps at once.

Epsilon was built to change that. We don't replace doctors. We help users explain symptoms in their own language and receive culturally familiar, safe guidance — with doctor escalation when it's truly needed.

⚗️ Our Approach to Care

We present three layers of guidance for every symptom set:

• Allopathic: Conventional medicine advice (OTC medications, when to visit a doctor, dosage basics)

• Ayurvedic: Traditional Indian remedies as informational support — not guaranteed treatments

• Home Remedies: Familiar, accessible first-step care (turmeric milk, warm compress, steam inhalation)

We never say "this will cure you." We say "here is safe guidance — please consult a doctor if it persists."
